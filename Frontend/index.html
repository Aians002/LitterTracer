About Litter-Tracer

Litter-Tracer is a cutting-edge platform dedicated to enhancing urban cleanliness and efficiency in garbage management. Our mission is to empower communities to tackle litter issues effectively through innovative technology solutions.

Our Goals

  • Reduce litter and improve cleanliness in urban areas.
  • Optimize garbage collection routes and schedules to maximize efficiency.
  • Promote recycling and sustainable waste management practices.
